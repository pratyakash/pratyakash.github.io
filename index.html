    Paper Recycling Machine

    In this Project, my role was Head Designer and I made the 3D design of a Paper Recycling Machine. This machine uses screw jack and hydraulic piston to compress the slurry of the paper and final product is the handmade paper which we can use post drying it. This machine can be used in schools, colleges and offices to recycle the waste paper with minor modification.
